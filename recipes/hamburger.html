Get ready, because you are about to make the burger of your dreams, people!

Here is what you will need.

  • Ground Chuck
  • Crushed Crackers
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Seasonings
  1. First Set out large bowl and add the ground beef,crackers,egg,sauce, and spices
  2. Use your hands (or mixer) and mix the ingrediants until they are smooth.
  3. Next, press the meat down until it makes an even disc and use a knife to divide the burger into 12 thin patties.
  4. Place patties on baking sheet lined with wax paper.
  5. Make a dent in the patties making it in the center of each patty
  6. Preheat the grill or skillet to a meduim heat or appoximately 350-400 degrees F.
    • I love using a cast iron skillet for getting that caramelized sear on the burgers.
